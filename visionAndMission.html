Our Vision



“To Gift the Nation with Eminent Engineers and Managers, Capable of Contributing Towards Technological Advancement and Betterment of the Society.”



Our Mission



  • To advance the well-being of society through excellence in teaching, research and service that exploits the rapidly changing technical diversity via a collaborative environment that stimulates faculty, staff and students to reach their highest potential through continuous learning.
  • Instill the foundation of professionalism and provide the tools required to advance the technological world.
  • Inculcate knowledge and ethics, and nurture/foster innovation and team man ship among the graduates/alumnae.
  • Endow eminent education to serve the society with graduates/alumnae possessing ace skills and potential.
  • Sustain highest reception of the institute’s alumnae among the stakeholders.
